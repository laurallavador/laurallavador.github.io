CompressFlow

Multimedia Motion Photography Brand

I designed CompressFlow, as a powerful tool within Webflow ecosystem, built to enhance and streamline web design processes. With an intuitive interface, and robust functionality, it allows users to convert, compress, and resize images efficiently, among many other capabilities. Perfect for creating faster, more optimized, and visually polished designs.

Go to the prototype →

Problem & Solution

Problem

Large images and manual optimizations slow down sites and create inconsistent visual results across devices.

Solution

CompressFlow automates conversion, resizing and quality tuning with a single, predictable workflow — saving time and improving performance.
