Case Overview

Brand Transformation Through Multimedia Design

Damel Group sought to reposition itself as a modern, energetic, and approachable brand while maintaining its heritage. My work focused on developing a consistent and appealing visual language across digital and offline touchpoints.

The Challenge

The main goal was to rejuvenate the brand and increase visibility across digital platforms. The brand needed to connect with younger audiences through a fresh identity and dynamic communication style.

My Role

As a Multimedia Designer, I worked on visual campaigns, internal communication materials, TV ads, and influencer merchandising — always ensuring alignment with the new tone of the brand.

Key Contributions

  • Designed and produced social media campaigns across Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook.
  • Created motion graphics and video ads for TV and online platforms.
  • Led the design of visual assets for internal communication and marketing.
  • Developed influencer kits and promotional merchandise.
  • Collaborated on the website’s visual updates and user experience improvements.

Results

Through a coherent and engaging visual strategy, Damel achieved a remarkable improvement in its digital visibility. The brand’s Instagram audience grew from 3,000 to 10,000 followers in less than a year — a direct result of consistent design, storytelling, and community engagement.

Learnings & Impact

This project reinforced my ability to combine design, storytelling, and strategy to drive measurable impact. It showed how design can be both creative and data-driven, playing a central role in shaping a brand’s evolution.
