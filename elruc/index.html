Sophisticated branding for a modern furniture company

Branding Graphic design

El Ruc is a brand created to give identity to a company that produces exclusive furniture. This brand combines sophistication and minimalism with a youthful, modern touch. In addition to values that highlight elegance, nature, formality, and craftsmanship, it also incorporates a modern flair that conveys trust and familiarity to the customer.
